Features

Everything you need to manage money smarter

From automatic SMS import to detailed analytics, Credzo packs powerful financial tools into a clean, intuitive interface.

📊

Smart Expense Tracking

Log expenses in seconds. Organise by category, credit card, date, and description. View real-time spending summaries per billing cycle or month.

  • ✓ Add, edit & delete expenses instantly
  • ✓ 11 default + custom categories
  • ✓ Per-card expense breakdown
💳

Credit Card Management

Link multiple credit cards (VISA, Mastercard, AMEX, RuPay) and track spending against each card's billing cycle independently.

🎯

Monthly Budget Limits

Set category-wise budget limits globally or per credit card. Get alerts before you overspend so you can course-correct in time.

📱

SMS Auto-Import

Credzo reads bank SMS on Android to automatically detect and import transactions — no manual entry needed for debit spends.

📈

Advanced Reports & Analytics

Visualise your financial story with beautiful charts. Track 12-month historical trends, category distribution pie charts, and planned vs. actual budget bars.

  • ✓ 12-month trend line charts
  • ✓ Category spending pie charts
  • ✓ Planned vs. actual budget bars
🔔

Smart Notifications

Customise spending alert frequency — every 12 hours, daily, weekly, or monthly. Get reminders before limits are hit.

🤖

AI-Powered Planner

ELITE members unlock personalised spend plans and AI budget insights powered by your real spending history.

🌍

Multi-Currency & Language

Track expenses in INR, USD, EUR, or JPY. Switch app language between English, Hindi, Spanish, and German.

How It Works

Up and running in under 2 minutes

Simple, intuitive, and powerful from day one.

01

Create Your Account

Sign up with email and set up your profile. Choose your preferred currency and language.

02

Add Your Credit Cards

Link your cards with billing dates. Credzo will group expenses per billing cycle automatically.

03

Set Category Budgets

Define monthly spending limits per category — Food, Shopping, Travel, and more.

04

Track & Grow

Log expenses (or let SMS auto-import do it), view reports, and refine your budget over time.
